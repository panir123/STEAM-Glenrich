About Start-Up Guru Club

The Startup Guru Club is a student run club dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial spirit and innovation. It serves as a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to explore business ideas, develop startups, and learn the essentials of launching and growing a successful company. Through hands-on activities, workshops, and guest lectures from industry professionals, the club provides members with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate the world of startups.


The club focuses on key aspects of entrepreneurship, including ideation, business planning, market research, product development, and fundraising. Members have the opportunity to pitch their business concepts, receive mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs, and collaborate with peers who share a passion for innovation. The Startup Guru Club is also a space for networking, helping students build connections with investors, startup founders, and other professionals in the business ecosystem.
