About MUN Club

The Model United Nations (MUN) Club is a dynamic student run club that simulates the proceedings of the United Nations. It offers members the opportunity to engage in debates, negotiations, and diplomacy on global issues such as human rights, international security, environmental sustainability, and economic development. In MUN, participants represent different countries and take on the roles of diplomats, working to draft resolutions, form alliances, and develop solutions to complex world problems.


The club helps students enhance their research, public speaking, leadership, and critical thinking skills. It's also a great space for those interested in international relations, political science, and diplomacy to deepen their understanding of global affairs. Through conferences, workshops, and interactive activities, the MUN Club fosters a collaborative and intellectually stimulating environment where students can expand their knowledge, build confidence, and become more effective communicators.
