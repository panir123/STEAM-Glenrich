About Wellness Club

The Wellness Club is a student club dedicated to promoting physical, mental, and emotional well-being within the school community. The club aims to create a supportive environment where students can learn about and practice healthy habits, self-care, and stress management. It focuses on holistic wellness, covering areas such as nutrition, fitness, mental health awareness, mindfulness, and overall personal development.


Through workshops, group discussions, fitness activities, and mindfulness sessions, the Wellness Club helps members explore different ways to maintain balance in their lives and improve their overall well-being. The club often hosts events like yoga classes, meditation sessions, mental health awareness campaigns, and wellness challenges. It also provides resources and tools to help students manage academic pressure, maintain a healthy lifestyle, and cultivate positive mental health practices.
