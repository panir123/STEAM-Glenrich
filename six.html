About Girl Up Club

The Girl Up Club is a student-led club dedicated to empowering girls and promoting gender equality. It is part of the global Girl Up movement, which is supported by the United Nations Foundation. The club focuses on advocacy, education, and community action, aiming to inspire and support young girls and women locally and globally. Members work together to raise awareness about issues such as gender-based violence, access to education, health rights, and leadership opportunities for girls.


Through campaigns, events, and workshops, the Girl Up Club engages members in meaningful discussions on gender equality and provides them with the tools to become advocates for change. The club also collaborates with other organizations and participates in fundraising efforts to support global initiatives that benefit girls in underserved communities. Additionally, members have the opportunity to develop leadership skills, participate in advocacy training, and connect with like-minded peers who are passionate about creating a more equitable world for women and girls.
