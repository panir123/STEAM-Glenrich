About Newspaper Club

The Newspaper Club is a student-led club dedicated to journalism, writing, and storytelling. It provides a platform for students to express their ideas, share news, and report on events happening within the school and the wider community. Through the club, members work together to produce the school newspaper, covering topics such as current events, school activities, student life, opinion pieces, sports, arts, and culture.


Members of the Newspaper Club develop skills in writing, editing, research, interviewing, and photography, all while learning the fundamentals of journalism. They collaborate to brainstorm story ideas, conduct interviews, fact-check information, and create engaging articles. The club also offers opportunities for students interested in design and layout, as they work on the visual presentation of the paper, whether in print or digital format.
