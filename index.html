Club Directory

MUN Club
The Model United Nations (MUN) club is built around the core ideals of United Nations who seek to simulate and embody diplomacy as its core value, this enhances their understanding of global relations.

Start-Up-Guru Club
Our club guides students through stages of startup development, especially those without technical expertise.Mentorship, product development help and support in the early stages; are some of the things this club offers.

Eco-Bizz CLub
The Eco-Bizz Club is a student-run club that enhances members' understanding of economic principles. Which is acheived through discussions, debates, and activities; along with various other events.

Student Council Club
Our Student Council Club represents the student body, allowing members to voice opinions for student interests. The council organizes events and initiatives to enhance the school environment and community spirit.

Newspaper Club
The Newspaper Club fosters journalism and writing skills. Members engage in activities like writing articles and producing content for the school newspaper. Here students express their views and enhance their understanding.

Girl Up Club
Our Girl Up Club empowers girls and advocates for gender equality through leadership skills, awareness campaigns, and community service activities; it also contribute to initiatives that support girls.

Coding & Graphics Club
The Coding and Graphics Club is a popular club that teaches coding and graphic design skills through hands-on projects and workshops, enhancing programming and creative abilities.

Well-Being Club
The Well-Being Club promotes mental and physical health by enhancing mindfulness and emotional resilience through workshops and group activities focused on self-care and stress reduction.

Community Club
The Community Club focuses on enhancing student engagement.It typically organizes events, service projects, and activities aimed at building connections among students while contributing positively to the community.

STEAM Club
The STEAM Club emphasizes the integration of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics. It aims to enhance students' critical thinking and creativity through hands-on projects and activities.

Drama Club
The Drama Club focuses on developing students' stage presence, communication, and teamwork skills. It offers opportunities for students to explore acting, theater production, and other performance arts.

Library & Literature Club
The Library and Literature Club combines a passion for reading with the exploration of literary works. It encourages students to engage in discussions about various genres and promotes a culture of reading.

Environmental Club
Our Environment Club is mainly focused on raising awareness about environmental issues and promoting sustainable practices within the school or community.

Debate Club
Our Debate Club is where participants can engage in structured and formal debates on a wide range of issues, including current events, ethical dilemmas, and global affairs; which are few of the many activites.

Olympiad Club
The Olympiad Club helps students prepare for a specific olympiad. Students engage in various activites and papers that prepare them for the actual test. The clubs subjects vary, from science all the way to bangla.

Acheivements

Community Club

Organized a successful fundraising campaign that raised significant funds for flood victims, providing vital resources such as food, shelter, and medical aid. This initiative involved local businesses and community members, underscoring our commitment to social responsibility.

MUN Club

Organizes annual Model United Nations (MUN) events that allow students to represent different countries and engage in discussions on global issues. These events enhance skills in diplomacy, critical thinking, and public speaking while fostering a vibrant environment for debate and collaboration among participants.

Girl-Up Club

Successfully raised a substantial amount of money to purchase and distribute sanitary products to women in need. This initiative ensured that women have access to essential hygiene products, which is crucial for their health and dignity
